    Simple and effective front pocket wallet
    By T. Simonson on January 21, 2013
    Started using money clips almost 30 years ago, and when I started to carry more cards around 22+ years ago, I switched to money clip card wallets. Having used money clips for a few years, I knew to avoid wallets that had a basic friction clip and stared with a front pocket wallet with a hinged clip. I probably would have continued with hinged clips until the day my wife gave me this Bosca magnetic clip wallet as a gift about 8 years ago. I absolutely loved it, and only replaced it after about 5 years because I had been carrying to many cards, which stretched the leather pockets. Unfortunately, I assumed every magnetic clip would work about the same and purchased a $30 Fossil version from the department store. I soon discovered the thinner magnets couldn't even hold half as many bills, and quickly replaced the Fossil with another Bosca.
