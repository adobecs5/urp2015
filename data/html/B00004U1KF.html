    Decent Halloween costume. Great for size 12/14/16
    By Allison on October 20, 2013
    Size: Standard (fits size 12-14)Color: Black Verified Purchase
    Let's be honest about Halloween costumes. They are designed to wear once or twice and they are made of cheap materials in China. This costume falls right into that category. The picture is a styled up version worn by a model, but it's not inaccurate. My advice is to lower your expectations with store bought Halloween costumes and they work out great.

    This fit me well and I am size 14-16. If you are a size 12 this will also work for you well. Made of rather thin polyester crushed velveteen, the edges of fabric aren't hemmed and it comes folded up in a bag so it's a bit wrinkled. Wash before wearing and hang to dry and it looks a lot better. The "snake" armband is the loser of this ensemble, as it does not stay on your arm. I wore as a necklace instead. The headpiece is pretty cool looking, but it is like those plastic masks you wore as a kid with elastic around the back of the head to keep in place. In other words, be gentle with the headpiece. I got the Medusa dreadlock auburn wig and slathered on makeup and this looked great as an ensemble.
