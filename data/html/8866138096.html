    A lot of protection for your teeny things
    By Ella Kwint on June 13, 2015
    Size: One SizeColor: Brilliant Violet Verified Purchase
    Despite acknowledging that Moleskine makes quality products that inspire intense brand loyalty, I've never hopped on the bandwagon; as a notebook enthusiast, I find most Moleskine items a tad overpriced. But when I spied this case on one of my favorite pen & paper websites, I was intrigued. I love all things 'mini' and am an organization junkie to boot. Amazon's price couldn't be beat so I took the plunge.

    First things first, when Moleskine calls this "XS" they really and truly mean EXTRA SMALL. Similar versions of this case are available in larger sizes so definitely peruse those if you need room for oodles of stuff or for an e-reader, camera, smart phone, etcetera. Double-check the external dimensions as Amazon has them listed under "Product Description" at mid page. My tape measure clocks them at approx 3.5" W x 5" H x 2" D. Deduct a bit for actual internal storage. Yep: Teeny!

    The possibilities with this case are endless if you carry a lot of tiny items (or just want to protect one or two important things). I ultimately purchased this to store travel-ready mini craft items I use for Fuji instant photography: business card-size blank card stock, mini scissors, mini pen, used film, sticker sheets, washi tape (flat pack), and a few other odds and ends. Yes, a person could use a little zippered pouch or pocket that would serve a similar purpose, but this product allows more for security, basic organization, and mobility.

    The back of the case has an adjustable attachment feature that allows you to affix the case to a strap--in my case, the handle of my camera bag.    Read more ›
