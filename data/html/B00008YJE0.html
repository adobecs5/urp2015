Amazing performance
By Billy on May 26, 2004
Format: Audio CD
Overall this CD is well worth buying... infact it would probably STILL be well worth buying at twice the price. First off I'd like to say that I personally know her sister: the secretary of our church, and her mom: one of the greatest piano teachers and performers. Second the performance is flawless, all pieces are played at the correct tempo with perfect articulation and phrasing. I would highly reccomend this CD, it's great no matter how familiar you are with the music. Margery Whatley puts on an outstanding performance.
