By kona111 on December 21, 2011
I absolutely love this purchase. The ears are a little smaller than in the picture, other than that - I'm super happy with this item! It's lightweight, yet warm - great for clubbing or to wear to the mall shopping etc. Great for a ski or snowboarding trip to wear around the lodge or under your winter jacket for layering. I recommend this item - they tend to be seasonal, so if you wait, they will be gone!
